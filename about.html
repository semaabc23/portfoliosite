01 : About me

Machinations of My Mind

I identify as a multimedia artist who dabbles with all ends of design. I have a huge interest in accessibility and aspire to create designs that are more accessible and consider the needs of a greater population.

When I'm Not Designing...

You can probably find me at a concert or an art museum. I also really enjoy nature and love going to the park in my free time with a good book.

My Background

I will graduate in May 2022 with a Bachelors in Science for Integrated Design Media and a minor in Science and Technology Studies. Throughout my four years at school, I have worked in game development, web development, film, photography, UX/UI, computer science, and graphic design.
